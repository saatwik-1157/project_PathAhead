Our Story

Built to close a real gap

Every year, thousands of Intermediate students in India choose streams, colleges and careers with very little real guidance. Decisions that shape their entire future are often made on hearsay, pressure or guesswork.

PathAhead was founded to change that. We bring practical, honest, industry-aware career guidance directly into schools and junior colleges — at exactly the moment students need it most.

2 hrs

that can reshape a student’s next 10 years

Our Mission

To empower every Intermediate student in India with the clarity, awareness and practical guidance they need to make confident, future-ready career decisions — before college.

Our Vision

A future where career guidance is a standard part of every student’s education — where choosing a path is driven by aptitude and awareness, not confusion or pressure.

Founder Story

Why we started PathAhead

After years of watching bright students struggle — not for lack of talent, but for lack of direction — our founder set out to build something better: guidance that reaches students early, speaks their language and reflects how careers actually work today.

PathAhead is that idea, brought to life across classrooms in India — one workshop, one student, one confident decision at a time.
