The one rule

Learn → practice in a lab → document it

Security is a hands-on craft. Everything below is practiced in your own legal lab (TryHackMe, HackTheBox). Only ever test systems you own or are authorized to test.

Important: Legal & ethical use only. Everything here is for authorized testing, your own lab, or educational platforms. Never attack systems you do not own or have explicit written permission to test — unauthorized access is a crime.
