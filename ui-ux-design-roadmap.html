Four phases, step by step

0

Design Foundations

Weeks 1–4

The visual and UX principles behind good design.

Week 1

Design principles

Hierarchy, contrast, alignment, balance.

Week 2

Color & typography

Palettes, type scales, readability.

Week 3

Layout & spacing

Grids, whitespace, visual rhythm.

Week 4

UX fundamentals

User-centered thinking, information architecture.

1

Tools & Process

Weeks 5–10

Master Figma and the end-to-end design flow.

Week 5

Figma basics

Frames, shapes, components, auto-layout.

Week 6

Wireframing

Low-fidelity structure before visuals.

Week 7

Prototyping

Clickable flows and interactions.

Week 8

Design systems

Reusable components and design tokens.

Week 9

User research

Interviews, surveys, personas.

Week 10 · Project ▶

App redesign

Redesign a real app and justify every choice.

2

Applied Design

Weeks 11–16

Design for real platforms and real users.

Week 11

Mobile patterns

Native iOS/Android conventions.

Week 12

Responsive web

Designing across screen sizes.

Week 13

Usability testing

Watch real users, find friction.

Week 14

Accessibility

Contrast, sizing, inclusive design.

Week 15

Developer handoff

Specs, assets, and clean delivery.

Week 16 · Project ▶

Full product design

An end-to-end product from research to UI.

3

Portfolio & Career

Weeks 17–20

Package your work to land the job.

Week 17

Case study writing

Tell the story: problem, process, outcome.

Week 18

Portfolio site

Present 3 strong pieces cleanly.

Week 19

Critique & iteration

Refine based on real feedback.

Week 20 · Capstone ▶

Polished portfolio

Three complete case studies, ready to share.
