The one rule

Prototype in 3D → test on device → optimize

XR only feels real on a device. Build small, run it on your phone (or headset) early, and optimize relentlessly — a smooth simple scene beats a beautiful slideshow every time.

Important: No headset needed to start: a recent Android phone covers all of mobile AR, and Unity's XR Device Simulator lets you build VR interactions before you ever touch a Quest.

The Roadmap

Four phases, step by step

0

Foundations

Weeks 1–5

C#, Unity, and thinking in three dimensions.

Week 1

C# basics

Variables, methods, classes — Unity's language.

Week 2

Unity editor

Scenes, GameObjects, components, play mode.

Week 3

3D intuition

Vectors, transforms, rotation — without heavy math.

Week 4

Physics & interaction

Rigidbodies, colliders, triggers, input.

Week 5

Git for Unity

Version control that survives binary assets.

1

Unity Depth

Weeks 6–11

From empty scene to polished mobile AR.

Week 6

UI & prefabs

Reusable objects and world-space interfaces.

Week 7

Animation & audio

Animator, timelines, spatial sound basics.

Week 8

Materials & lighting

Make scenes look good on a budget.

Week 9

AR Foundation

Plane detection and anchors on your own phone.

Week 10

AR interaction

Place, scale, and manipulate virtual objects.

Week 11 · Project ▶

AR placement app

A furniture/poster previewer running on your phone.

2

VR Development

Weeks 12–17

Presence, hands, and comfort — real VR skills.

Week 12

XR Interaction Toolkit

Rigs, controllers, grabbing and UI in VR.

Week 13

Locomotion

Teleport vs smooth movement — comfort first.

Week 14

Hands & interaction design

Natural grabbing, poking, and feedback.

Week 15

Standalone optimization

Hit frame rate on Quest-class hardware.

Week 16

Polish & haptics

Sound, vibration, and juice that sells presence.

Week 17 · Project ▶

VR experience

A room-scale game or training scene (simulator OK).

XR UX rules

Week 19

Profiling

A browser-based XR scene anyone can open.

Week 21

Publishing

Play Store for AR; App Lab route for VR.

Week 22 · Capstone ▶
