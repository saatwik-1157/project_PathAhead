The one rule

Build on testnets → verify on-chain → document

Web3 is learned by shipping contracts, not watching charts. Everything below is deployed to free test networks, verified publicly, and written up on GitHub — real proof anyone can check on-chain.

Important: This roadmap teaches blockchain development, not crypto trading. Buying, trading, or speculating on tokens is risky and regulated differently across countries — build the technology; don't gamble on it. Everything here uses free test networks.

Blockchain mechanics

Blocks, hashing, consensus, why it can't be quietly edited.

Cryptography basics

Public/private keys, signatures, hashes in practice.

Week 4

JavaScript refresher

The language of Web3 tooling and frontends.

Week 5

Smart-contract concepts

What contracts can (and can't) do; reading real ones.
