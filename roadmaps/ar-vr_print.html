The One Rule

Prototype in 3D → test on device → optimize

XR only feels real on a device. Build small, run it on your phone (or headset) early, and optimize relentlessly — a smooth simple scene beats a beautiful slideshow every time.

Legal & ethical use only. No headset needed to start: a recent Android phone covers all of mobile AR, and Unity's XR Device Simulator lets you build VR interactions before you ever touch a Quest.
